The AAO is the world's largest association of ophthalmologists. Their website provides comprehensive information on eye diseases, conditions, and treatments.

National Eye Institute (NEI)

The NEI, part of the National Institutes of Health, conducts and supports research on eye diseases and vision disorders. Their website offers patient education materials and information on clinical trials.
